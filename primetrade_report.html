🔎 Hidden Patterns & Insights

1. Fear Zones Drive Volume, Not Just Panic

Fear periods account for $483M in trading volume (41% of all volume) — the highest of any sentiment. Traders are most active during fear, contradicting the idea that fear drives inactivity.

2. Weak Correlation Between FG Index and Daily PnL

Pearson r = 0.037 — almost no linear relationship between the raw FG Index score and daily average PnL. This means sentiment alone doesn't predict daily performance; how traders respond to sentiment matters more than the sentiment itself.

3. Contrarian Traders Outperform in Extreme Fear

Top 5 traders in Extreme Fear earned between $74K–$262K individually. These traders actively positioned long during the market's most fearful moments — a classic contrarian alpha strategy.

4. Trade Size Shrinks in Extreme Greed

Average trade size drops from $7,816 (Fear) to $3,112 (Extreme Greed) — a 60% reduction. Smart money appears to scale down position sizes and take profits during euphoric markets.

5. Fee Drag is Minimal but Real

Total fees of $245K represent 2.39% of gross PnL ($10.3M). For high-frequency traders, fee optimization could meaningfully improve net returns.

6. December 2024 Was the Greed Goldmine

December 2024 saw $2.01M PnL in Extreme Greed and $990K in Greed — the single best month. This coincides with post-election BTC rally euphoria. Traders who shorted into this rally captured massive gains.

🚀 Strategic Recommendations

Signal 1 — Fear = Buy Signal: Deploy long positions when FG Index < 30. Historical avg PnL per trade is $54–67 vs $34 in neutral markets.
Signal 2 — Extreme Greed = Sell/Short Signal: SELL-side trades during Extreme Greed yield $114.58 avg — the single highest across all categories.
Signal 3 — Focus on @107, HYPE, SOL: These three coins account for $6.37M of total $10.3M PnL. Sentiment-timed entries on these assets maximise alpha.
Signal 4 — Scale Down in Greed: Reduce position sizes as FG approaches 80+. Data confirms smart money already does this (avg size drops 60%).
Risk Note: Win rates never exceed 47% across any sentiment — meaning most individual trades close at zero or loss. PnL is driven by a small number of large winning trades. Risk management and position sizing are critical.
